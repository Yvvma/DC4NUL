Usually i tend to approach a project, generally through basic steps:

  • Communication

    First, I try to understand the problem or identify the needs of the clients or users. This is one of the most important steps in creating a solution.

  • Ressearch

    Next, I delve into my own research, exploring examples and gathering data from the client. This allows me to bridge the gap between the product and the customer's needs.

  • Prototype

    With all the gathered information, I proceed to prototype, mapping out the features we can implement into the project before presenting it to the client.
